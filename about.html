ABOUT

Welcome to Pine City Mall, your premier shopping destination in the heart of the city. Our mission is to provide a vibrant and diverse shopping experience that caters to the needs and preferences of our esteemed visitors. With an extensive selection of top-notch retail brands, delightful dining options, and exciting entertainment offerings, Pine City Mall is committed to creating memorable moments for shoppers of all ages.

HISTORY

Established in 1990, Pine City Mall has been a pillar of the community for over three decades. Since its inception, the mall has undergone significant transformations and expansions, continuously evolving to meet the growing demands of our discerning customers. Our commitment to excellence has earned us a reputation as a leading retail destination, attracting locals and visitors alike with our exceptional shopping experience and world-class amenities. In recent years, Pine City Mall underwent a major renovation, enhancing its architectural design and introducing innovative features to ensure a contemporary and inviting atmosphere for all.
